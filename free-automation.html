A free automation isn't an enterprise build. It's one trigger, one action, one contained workflow, the same as what we call a Tier 1 in our pricing. That's the scope of what's free.

If what you actually need is bigger, we'll tell you that on the first call and give you a straight number before you decide anything.

What happens after

Nothing automatically. The free build isn't a trial that converts to billing. It's just a free build.

If it's useful and you want more, we can add automations at flat, predictable pricing starting at $750. If you want us to run your outreach or pipeline alongside the system, that's a separate conversation we can have then.
