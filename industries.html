Industries

We go deep, not wide.

We work across three industries where we have real proof, real relationships, and a genuine understanding of how the sales motion actually works. If you're outside these three, we're still worth a conversation, but these are where we're best.

Industry 1

Real Estate

We've built and maintained AI systems for real estate brands including Offerpad and Realty ONE Group for over a year. The COO of a publicly traded real estate company and the VP of Growth Marketing at an 18,000-agent franchise have both trusted us with real work and real money. That's not a case study we wrote. That's a relationship that held.

We understand the MLS, the disclosure language, the fair housing exposure, and the way real estate sales actually work, which is different from generic B2B sales in ways that matter when you're building the system behind someone's pipeline.

What we build for real estate: lead qualification and scoring, listing and property marketing content, outreach and follow-up automation, investor and LP communications, HubSpot and CRM implementation, and embedded SDR work for brokerages and developers who need pipeline, not just tools.

Who we work with: agents and brokers, residential and commercial, developers, property managers, investors and funds, lenders and mortgage brokers.

Industry 2

B2B Technology & SaaS

B2B SaaS sales has a specific shape: longer cycles, multiple stakeholders, outreach that has to be personalized enough to cut through but scalable enough to cover a real market. Generic automation breaks down here because the judgment calls matter more than the volume.

We build around the specifics of how SaaS companies actually sell, the ICP scoring, the multi-touch sequences, the content that supports a discovery call, the CRM hygiene that makes forecasting something you can trust. And when we run outreach, we use Commonality to find the warm paths your team already has into the accounts you're targeting, which changes the conversion rate considerably.

What we build for B2B SaaS: ICP scoring and lead qualification, personalized outreach sequences, HubSpot implementation and optimization, pipeline reporting, content automation, and embedded SDR work for teams that need pipeline generation without adding headcount.

Who we work with: seed through Series B companies building a repeatable sales motion, growth-stage teams hitting the limits of what founder-led sales can do, and RevOps teams that need the infrastructure to match the ambition.

Industry 3

Professional Services

Professional services firms, consultancies, advisory practices, agencies, sell through relationships and reputation. The sales motion is slower, more personal, and almost entirely dependent on trust. Which means the AI has to be invisible and the human judgment has to be front and center.

We don't build spray-and-pray outreach for professional services firms. We build systems that help you identify who in your existing network is most likely to become a client right now, what to say to them, and how to stay visible to the right people without feeling like you're selling. That's what Commonality was built for, and it's where we see the highest response rates and the most durable relationships.

What we build for professional services: network and social capital mapping, warm outreach systems, referral and relationship tracking, proposal and follow-up automation, CRM implementation for relationship-driven teams, and embedded business development work for firms that need someone to actually do the outreach.

Who we work with: boutique consultancies, advisory firms, agencies, coaches, and fractional executives who sell through relationships and need a better system for finding and warming the right ones.
