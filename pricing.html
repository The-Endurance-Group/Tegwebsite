What this looks like in practice

  • A new lead fills out your website form and gets automatically added to HubSpot, tagged by source, and assigned to the right rep.
  • A Claude skill that reads a prospect's LinkedIn URL and drafts a personalized first-touch outreach email before your team opens their inbox.
  • A HubSpot workflow that enrolls a lead based on behavior, sends a timed sequence of three personalized follow-ups, and notifies the rep when the lead replies.
  • A lead comes in through your website, gets enriched with company and contact data from Apollo, scored against your ICP criteria, added to HubSpot with full context, and triggers a personalized outreach sequence, all without a human touching it.
  • A system that pulls weekly sales activity from HubSpot, runs it through a Claude skill to generate a narrative performance summary, and emails it to leadership every Monday morning.
  • A Commonality-powered outreach system that maps your team's social capital against a target prospect list, identifies warm paths through shared schools and past employers, and drafts personalized outreach for each rep anchored to the specific connection they share.
