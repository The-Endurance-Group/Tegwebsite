Every automation we build falls into one of four tiers based on what it actually involves. Here's what each tier looks like in practice.

Tier 1

To build$750
To maintain$200/mo

One trigger, one action, a single tool.

  • A new lead fills out your website form and gets automatically added to HubSpot, tagged by source, and assigned to the right rep.
  • A Claude skill that reads a prospect's LinkedIn URL and drafts a personalized first-touch outreach email before your team opens their inbox.
  • A Zap that catches a won deal in your CRM and kicks off a congratulations email to the client the same day.
  • A Custom GPT trained on your FAQ that answers inbound product questions in your brand voice.

Tier 2

To build$1,450
To maintain$350/mo

A few connected steps inside one system.

  • A Gem that takes a property address and basic details and returns five ready-to-post listing captions, one per platform, formatted and hashtag-ready.
  • A Claude skill that reads a new lead's intake form, scores their fit against your ICP, and drafts a qualification email with a recommended next step.
  • A HubSpot workflow that enrolls a lead based on behavior, sends a timed sequence of three personalized follow-ups, and notifies the rep when the lead replies.
  • A Custom GPT trained on your sales deck and case studies that preps your team with talking points before any discovery call.

Tier 3

To build$2,800
To maintain$725/mo

A multi-step workflow crossing two or three systems.

  • A lead comes in through your website, gets enriched with company and contact data from Apollo, scored against your ICP criteria, added to HubSpot with full context, and triggers a personalized outreach sequence, all without a human touching it.
  • A Claude skill that reads a new lease document, abstracts the key dates, rent steps, and renewal options, and pushes a structured summary into your CRM and a shared Slack channel.
  • A Zap-plus-Claude workflow that monitors a prospect list for job changes, funding announcements, or new hires, drafts a signal-based outreach email for each trigger, and queues it for rep review before sending.
  • A system that pulls weekly sales activity from HubSpot, runs it through a Claude skill to generate a narrative performance summary, and emails it to leadership every Monday morning.

Tier 4

To build$5,500
To maintain$1,400/mo

A complex build spanning multiple systems and steps.

  • A full inbound pipeline: leads captured from multiple sources, enriched, scored, routed to the right rep by territory and deal size, enrolled in the right sequence, and logged in Salesforce, with a real-time Slack alert to the rep and a daily digest to leadership.
  • A Custom GPT trained on your fund's historical reporting templates, deal memos, and investor communications that turns raw quarterly numbers into a polished, ready-to-send LP update in your voice.
  • A Commonality-powered outreach system that maps your team's social capital against a target prospect list, identifies warm paths through shared schools and past employers, and drafts personalized outreach for each rep anchored to the specific connection they share.
  • A multi-system sales intelligence dashboard that pulls from your CRM, your email tool, LinkedIn activity, and enrichment data to surface at-risk deals, stalled leads, and best next actions, updated daily without anyone touching a spreadsheet.
