Then, one of two things happens

We decide together on the call. Here's what each path actually looks like.

We build you a free automation

A working automation built around something your team actually does today, installed and running before any money changes hands. Capped at 5 hours of build time and one contained workflow. A few examples of what that's looked like:

  • A new lead automatically routed, tagged, and assigned in HubSpot
  • A Claude skill that drafts a personalized first-touch email from a LinkedIn URL
  • A Zap that kicks off onboarding the moment a contract is signed

We run a free private training

A focused, one-hour session for you or your team on whatever tool will actually move the needle. We pick the topic together on the call. A few examples of what that's looked like:

  • How to build and use Claude Skills
  • How to create a Custom GPT
  • How to set up an automation in Zapier

No cost, no contract, either way

This isn't a trial that converts to billing. You don't pay for the call, the automation, or the training, and there's no obligation to continue after.

If it's useful and you want more, we can add automations at flat, predictable pricing starting at $750, or schedule more training as needed. If it's not useful or we're not a fit, you keep what we built or taught and owe us nothing.
