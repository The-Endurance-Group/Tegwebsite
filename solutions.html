How We Build

We build in the systems and workflows where your team already work.

Tools connected without complexity

Most AI projects require integration layers that need their own maintenance. We connect the systems you already have - your CRM, email, spreadsheets, data - directly through the AI. Everything works together without building something fragile that needs constant upkeep.

Built on proven infrastructure

The more custom a piece of software is, the more can break. By building inside established AI platforms, we're building on infrastructure that thousands of engineers maintain and improve every day. Your automations benefit from that stability without you paying for it.

Teams actually use it

The biggest reason AI projects fail is adoption. When the automation lives inside a tool people already use, there's no barrier to entry. Nothing to install, nothing to learn, nothing new to remember. They just use Claude, Hubspot, Gmail, or whatever other system the same way they always did- except now it does more.
