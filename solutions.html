Solution 1

AI Automations

We build custom automations that handle the sales and marketing work your team avoids or can't keep up with. Lead qualification, outreach, follow-up, reporting, content, the repetitive work that eats your team's time and never quite gets done consistently.

Every automation is built around how your team actually works, not a generic template with your logo on it. We pick the right tool for the job, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Zapier, HubSpot, or something else, and we install it with you so it's actually running before we leave.

What this looks like: a lead comes in through your website, gets enriched and scored automatically, routed to the right rep, and dropped into a personalized outreach sequence before anyone on your team has opened their laptop.

Solution 2

Sales Execution

We step in as your sales team. Prospecting, outreach, follow-up, booking meetings, working the pipeline. Not a system that runs on autopilot and hopes for the best. A person doing the work alongside AI, which is why it actually converts.

We use Commonality, our proprietary social capital tool, to find warm paths into your target prospects through shared schools, past employers, and mutual connections before we send a single message. That's why our outreach produces results like FenestraPro's 30% average response rate, not because we send more, because we send the right message to the right person at the right time.

What this looks like: we take your target account list, map your team's shared history against every prospect, identify the warmest paths in, and run a personalized outreach motion that opens real conversations instead of getting ignored.
